Greetings from the Organizing Committee

Dear Esteemed Friends & Colleagues,

It brings us immense joy to extend a heartfelt invitation to the 53rd Annual Conference of the Endocrine Society of India (ESICON 2024), set to take place in the beautiful city of Visakhapatnam.

This prestigious event promises to deliver an enriching academic banquet, showcasing the latest advancements in the dynamic field of Endocrinology. With a harmonious blend of foundational knowledge and cutting-edge innovations, the conference aims to captivate and inspire attendees. Nestled in the heart of Andhra Pradesh, Visakhapatnam, affectionately known as Vizag, City of Destiny, stands as a beacon of historical significance and natural splendor. Renowned for its breathtaking beaches, tranquil landscapes, and the majestic Eastern Ghats embracing its surroundings, Visakhapatnam offers an idyllic setting for a truly unforgettable coastal retreat. We eagerly anticipate your esteemed presence, accompanied by your beloved family and colleagues, to actively engage in what promises to be a captivating and enlightening experience. Prepare to embark on a journey filled with cherished moments and invaluable insights.

With warm regards

ESICON 2024 Organizing Committee
